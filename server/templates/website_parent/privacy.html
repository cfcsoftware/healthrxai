Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how HealthRx Technologies Private Limited (“Healthsrx”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our web and mobile applications (“Platforms”) and services (“Services”).

By accessing or using our Platforms or Services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you disagree with any part of this Policy, please do not use the Platforms or Services.


1. Consent

When you use our Services or voluntarily provide personal information, you consent to our collection, processing, and sharing of your data as outlined in this Policy. This includes sensitive personal information such as medical records, biometric data, and financial details, in compliance with applicable laws including the Information Technology Act and its allied rules.


2. Who This Policy Applies To

This Policy applies to:

  • Doctors: Including registered medical practitioners using our platform to consult patients.
  • Patients: Individuals using our Services for consultations, health monitoring, or medical records.
  • Employees & Interns: Individuals employed or interning with us who interact with personal data.
  • Vendors & Partners: Third parties with contractual relationships involving data exchange.
  • Visitors: Users who visit the Platform but may not be registered.

3. Information We Collect

We collect various categories of personal and non-personal data:

  • Identity data: Full name, date of birth, photo ID, gender
  • Contact details: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Health data: Medical history, prescriptions, test results, clinical notes
  • Financial data: Transaction records, payment card details (processed securely)
  • Technical data: IP address, device ID, browser type, operating system, logs
  • Behavioral data: Search history, clickstream, interactions on the platform
  • Geolocation data: When enabled, used for regional services like vaccine slots

4. How We Use Your Information

Your information is processed for the following purposes:

  • To register you on our Platforms
  • To facilitate doctor-patient consultations and diagnostic services
  • To securely store and manage medical records
  • To communicate important updates and health reminders
  • To process payments and manage billing
  • To improve Services through analytics and feedback
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

5. Google API Data

We integrate with Google APIs to enable features like syncing your health appointments with your Gmail calendar. Access is limited, read-only, and conforms to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. We do not access, store, or share your Gmail content without your permission.


6. Mobile App Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions with your consent:

  • Camera and Microphone: For telemedicine consultations and uploading medical documents.
  • Storage Access: To read or upload health-related files.
  • Location Access: To offer location-specific services like clinic availability or vaccination drives (this data is not stored unless explicitly permitted).

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze web traffic. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit certain features of our Services. Third-party cookies (e.g., analytics or ad partners) are subject to their respective privacy policies.


8. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We may share your data with:

  • Authorized healthcare professionals, for service delivery
  • Third-party service providers under strict data processing agreements
  • Government, legal, or regulatory authorities when mandated
  • In case of business mergers, transfers, or acquisitions with appropriate safeguards

9. Data Security

Your data’s safety is our priority. We implement advanced security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, access control, periodic audits, and anonymization. Despite our best efforts, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage users to follow safe practices like setting strong passwords.


10. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request access to or correction of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where applicable)
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational considerations
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • File complaints with relevant data protection authorities

To exercise your rights, email us at care@healthsrx.com.


11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, regulatory, and operational requirements. Aggregated or anonymized data may be retained for analytics or research indefinitely.


12. Data Confidentiality

All personal information is handled with strict confidentiality. Healthsrx employees, vendors, and partners are bound by confidentiality agreements. We do not sell, lease, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.


13. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18 without parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without proper authorization.


14. International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses, are in place to maintain data protection levels in compliance with applicable laws.


15. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revision date. Continued use of our Services implies acceptance of any such changes.


16. Grievance Redressal Mechanism

If you have concerns, complaints, or questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our Grievance Officer:

  • Name: Grievance Officer
  • Designation: Grievance Officer
  • Email: care@healthsrx.com

We aim to resolve all complaints in a timely and fair manner.


For general privacy-related queries, reach out to us at care@healthsrx.com.
