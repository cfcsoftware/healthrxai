About Us

AI-Powered Platform for Smarter Consultations

HealthRx is an innovative hospital consultation system built with AI integration at its core. It empowers doctors with real-time tools to provide faster and better care while giving patients a smooth and transparent consultation experience.

Mission

Smarter Care. Seamless Consultations.

At HealthRx, our mission is to revolutionize the way hospitals manage doctor-patient consultations by integrating intelligent, AI-powered solutions.
